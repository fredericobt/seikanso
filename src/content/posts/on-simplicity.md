Simplicity is not about having less. It's about making room for what matters.

When you strip away the unnecessary, what remains becomes sharper. More intentional. More yours.

## The Cost of Complexity

Every feature added is a feature that needs explaining, maintaining, and eventually removing. Every extra word dilutes the ones that count.

The Kanso theme is named after a Japanese aesthetic principle: simplicity, the elimination of what is unnecessary.

## In Practice

Start with one thing. Do it well. Then ask whether the next thing is truly needed.

More often than not, the answer is no.
