Every craftsperson has their kit. Here's mine, as it stands today.

## Writing

**Obsidian** for long-form thinking and notes. The local-first approach matters to me — my words live on my machine first.

**iA Writer** when I need distraction-free focus. The typography alone is worth it.

## Development

**VS Code** with a minimal setup. I resist plugins until the pain of not having one is undeniable.

**Ghostty** as my terminal. Fast, native, no nonsense.

## Hardware

A plain desk. A good chair. Natural light when I can get it.

The tools fade. The thinking remains.
